What I'm doing now?

I'm spending most of my time in Somerville, MA climbing at Brookyln Boulders and working at Barrett Technology a robotics company in Newton, MA. I have been taking up more running, hiking and other outdoor activities besides climbing do to a string of minor injuries wich have been keeping me off of the ground. I am also starting to work on some projects.

My priorities are:

  • Working as an Embedded Systems Engineer at Barrett Technology
  • Recovering from minor injuries so that I can enjoy climbing, and hiking this wonderful Fall season. I want to get up the Whitney Gillman Ridge (5.7).
  • Starting to read more frequently again. I just finished The Diamond Age, and plan on reading Breakfast of Champions and Foundation and Empire next.
  • Appreciating life, journaling, meditating and building a positive life philosophy.
  • Finishing an LED project for a tap dance my significant other is working on. It involves Arduinos, nrf24 radios and some RGB LED strips. I am excited.
