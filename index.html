About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineer currently studying at Boston University and working on instrumentation for physics experiments with the Boston Universtiy Electronic Design Facility.

I catalogue my engineering projects at Happy Robot Labs with a focus on Open Source Hardware where applicable. Sharing my projects and interest in electronics and engineering is important to me. In addition to Happy Robot Labs, I contribute my talents to various outreach programs, such as, BU's Artemis Project, Sparkfun's soldering workshops and BU's student-run hackerspace BUILDS.
