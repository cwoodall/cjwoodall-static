Who am I?

I am an electrical and embedded systems engineer, rock climber, musician and a life long learner. Currently I work in the sprouting robotics industry in the Boston, Massachusetts area, but am a New York native.

I catalogue my personal projects at Happy Robot Labs with a focus on open source hardware and software, where applicable. To me sharing my projects is important as it helps encourage others in the community to work on the ideas that float through their minds. I also maintain a currated portfolio and Github.

When I am not tinkering or working I am climbing rocks, playing the mandolin, or reading
